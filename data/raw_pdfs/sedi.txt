## 1. DIRECTIVE ON STUDENT TRANSFER (EU-DIR-017-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 31.03.2021\
\
**Purpose:** Regulates procedures for student transfers within EPOKA University and from other higher education institutions.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Application Period:** Must be submitted during specified academic calendar period\
- **Decision Time:** Within one week after document submission\
- **Intra-University Transfer:**\
  - Bachelor's: Minimum 2 semesters completed\
  - Master's: Minimum 1 semester completed\
  - Minimum grade requirements: 1.00/4.00 (Bachelor's), 2.50/4.00 (Master's)\
- **Inter-University Transfer:**\
  - From other universities: Minimum 2 semesters completed\
  - Credit recognition limits: 2/3 (Bachelor's), 3/4 (Integrated Master's)\
- **Requirements:**\
  - No transfers during first/second/final semesters\
  - No disciplinary record\
  - English proficiency certificate required\
  - Only one transfer allowed per student\
\
## 2. DIRECTIVE ON THE PROMOTION OF SCIENTIFIC PUBLICATIONS (EU-DIR-004-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 04.06.2025\
\
**Purpose:** Encourages academic staff to publish and participate in scientific activities.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Support Commission:** Appointed by Rectorate annually\
- **Reward Calculation:** Based on points \'d7 financial coefficient\
- **Publication Categories:**\
  - SCI/SSCI/AHCI journals: Points based on quartile and Impact Factor\
  - Scopus journals: 300 points\
  - Books: 300-500 points based on publisher ranking\
  - Patents: 1000-2000+ points\
- **Author Share:**\
  - 2 authors: 60%/40%\
  - 3 authors: 50%/30%/20%\
  - 4+ authors: 40%/30%/20%/10% (remaining)\
- **Eligibility:** Only publications under EPOKA University name\
- **Internal Collaboration Required:** \uc0\u8805 2 EPOKA staff for journal articles\
- **Conference Support:** For international conferences indexed in Scopus/Web of Science\
- **Annual Cap:** Reward \uc0\u8804  one month's gross salary (excluding patents)\
\
## 3. DIRECTIVE ON STUDENT CLUBS (EU-DIR-005-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 31.03.2021\
\
**Purpose:** Regulates establishment, organization and functioning of student clubs.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Establishment:** Minimum 6 students + EPOKA staff advisor\
- **Activities:** One academic year duration\
- **Membership:** Open only to students; alumni as honor members\
- **Organs:**\
  - Dean of Students: Approves requests, programs, changes\
  - Club Advisor: Monitors establishment and functioning\
  - Club Board: Administrative board (President, VP, Treasurer, etc.)\
  - Student Clubs and Activities Office: Audits, prepares reports\
- **Documents Required:** Registration list, meeting register, materials registration\
- **Finances:** Small cash allowed; all expenses/revenues must be documented\
- **Closure:** For rule violations or inactivity (2 months without activities)\
\
## 4. DIRECTIVE ON THE FUNCTIONING OF THE EXAM BOARD (EU-DIR-020-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 15.12.2020\
\
**Purpose:** Determines duties of Exam Board to ensure exam quality.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Composition:** Chairperson + at least one representative from each Faculty\
- **Duties:**\
  - Establish exam guidelines\
  - Monitor exam organization and invigilation\
  - Ensure compliance with schedule\
  - Monitor quality assurance\
  - Evaluate fraud cases\
  - Evaluate grade appeals\
  - Review grade statistics\
  - Prepare annual report\
- **Expert for Study Program:** Assesses exam quality (at least one exam per semester)\
- **Meetings:** At least 2 general meetings + 4 semester meetings\
- **Annual Report:** Includes activities, grade distribution, fraud cases, improvement proposals\
\
## 5. LIBRARY DIRECTIVE (EU-DIR-002-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 14.09.2007\
\
**Purpose:** Enables users to benefit from library resources and protects these resources.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Users:** Students, staff, and approved guest users\
- **Rules Inside Library:** No food/drinks, no mobile phones, protect personal property\
- **Borrowing:**\
  - Students: 3-5 items for 15-20 days\
  - Staff: 5 items for 20 days\
  - Magazines: 2 items for 5 days\
- **Overdue Fees:** Calculated per day per item\
- **Lost/Damaged Resources:** Must pay replacement cost + overdue fees\
- **Sanctions:**\
  - No borrowing with unpaid debts\
  - Membership frozen for 2 years for irreplaceable rare resources\
  - Banned for 1 year for improper behavior\
\
## 6. DIRECTIVE ON ASSIGNMENT TO DUTY (EU-DIR-007-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 08.10.2009\
\
**Purpose:** Regulates task assignment of academic staff within and abroad.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Temporary Duty:** \uc0\u8804 1 month for scientific meetings/research\
  - \uc0\u8804 1 week: Department Head + Dean approval\
  - >15 days: Rector approval\
  - Apply 10-20 days in advance\
- **Long-term Duty:** >1 month for research/graduate studies\
  - Minimum 3 years as academic staff + 1 year teaching\
  - \uc0\u8804 1 year with remuneration (can extend 1 year without)\
- **Research Assistants:** Up to 3 years for graduate studies\
- **Compulsory Service:** Twice the assignment term; compensation if not fulfilled\
- **Reports:** Quarterly reports for graduate studies; final report before duty ends\
\
## 7. REGULATION ON PERMISSIONS (EU-REG-015-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 17.06.2016\
\
**Purpose:** Regulates permission in/out of country for academic and administrative staff.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Permission Types:**\
  - Yearly Leave: 28 days (Albanian staff), 35 days (foreign staff)\
  - Justified Permission: Marriage (5-10 days), birth (2-7 days), death (5-7 days)\
  - Medical Permission: Up to 6 months\
  - Maternity Leave: 126 paid days (70 before + 56 after birth)\
  - Unpaid Permission: Up to 6 months for family care\
  - Daily Permission: Up to 1 day\
- **Approval Authorities:** Rector, Dean, Secretary General based on duration and reason\
- **Penalties:** Wage deductions for unauthorized absences\
- **No Permission During:** Academic calendar except with adjusted teaching process\
\
## 8. REGULATION ON SECOND CYCLE STUDY PROGRAMS (EU-REG-017-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 06.03.2025\
\
**Purpose:** Determines functioning of Master's study programs.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Program Types:**\
  - Master of Science: 2 years, 120 ECTS, thesis required\
  - Professional Master: 1 year, 60 ECTS, microthesis required\
  - Integrated Second Cycle: 5 years, 300 ECTS\
- **Admission Requirements:**\
  - First/Second cycle diploma\
  - English proficiency: B2 (Master of Science), B1 (Professional Master)\
  - Minimum CGPA determined by Decanate\
- **Attendance:** 60% lectures, 75% practice, 75% laboratories\
- **Grading:** AA-FF scale; minimum CC to pass; CGPA \uc0\u8805 2.00 to graduate\
- **Thesis:**\
  - Master of Science: 4th semester with professional practice\
  - Professional Master: 2nd semester microthesis\
- **Student Rights:** Clear rules, non-discrimination, use of facilities, free expression\
- **Student Responsibilities:** Comply with regulations, ethical behavior, timely payments\
\
## 9. REGULATION ON SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH (EU-REG-038-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 15.12.2020\
\
**Purpose:** Regulates organization of scientific research activities.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Research Activities:** Basic/applied research, conferences, publications, patents\
- **Organization:** Coordinated by Research and Projects Office\
- **Funding:** University budget + external partners\
- **Individual Budget Lines:**\
  - International conference participation\
  - Publication in indexed journals\
- **Project Compensation:** Staff costs minus 30% institutional expenses\
- **Best Researcher Award:** Annual award determined by Rectorate\
- **Publications:** Regulated by Directive on Promotion of Scientific Publications\
\

## 11. REGULATION ON PHD STUDY PROGRAMS (EU-REG-034-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 24.10.2018\
\
**Purpose:** Defines rules for PhD program functioning.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Admission Requirements:**\
  - Master's degree or equivalent\
  - CGPA \uc0\u8805 2.70/4.00\
  - English proficiency C1 level\
  - PhD proposal in approved research area\
- **Program Structure:**\
  - Stage 1: Theoretical training (60 ECTS, 1 year)\
  - Stage 2: Dissertation writing\
- **Duration:** 3-5 years full-time\
- **Supervision:**\
  - Professor: \uc0\u8804 7 students\
  - Associate Professor: \uc0\u8804 5 students\
  - Part-time: \uc0\u8804 3 students\
- **Publication Requirements Before Defense:**\
  - 3 presentations (2 international)\
  - 3 journal articles (2 in indexed journals)\
- **PhD Jury:** 5 members (\uc0\u8805 3 Professors)\
- **Honorary Doctorate:** \uc0\u8804 2 per year for distinguished persons\
\
## 12. REGULATION ON PUBLICATIONS (EU-REG-013-EN)\
**Last Updated:** 14.09.2007\
\
**Purpose:** Regulates publications issued by EPOKA University units.\
\
### Key Points:\
- **Publication Commission:** 2 Administrative Board members + Vice-Rector chair\
- **Copyright Payment:** Based on page count and civil servant coefficient\
  - Textbooks: 175 points\
  - Auxiliary textbooks: 125 points\
  - Translation: 100-125 points\
- **Distribution:** Free copies to Ministry, libraries, teaching staff\
- **Sales:** Minimum 20% discount to students\
- **Subsequent Editions:** 3/5 of first edition copyright\
- **Cost Coverage:** University budget or circulating capital company\
