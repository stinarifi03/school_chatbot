## 1. Regulation on the Center for European Studies (EUCES)\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56516  Document Information\
- **Code:** EU-REG-009-EN\
- **Publication Date:** 14.09.2007\
- **Pages:** 3\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57263  Purpose and Scope\
- **Purpose:** Defines purpose, activities, governing bodies, tasks, and basic working methods of EUCES\
- **Scope:** Covers provisions on activities, administrative bodies, powers, and decision-making methods\
- **Legal Basis:** Statute of Epoka University\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56523  Key Definitions\
| Term | Definition |\
|------|------------|\
| University | Epoka University |\
| Rector | Rector of Epoka University |\
| Center | Center on European Studies (EUCES) |\
| Director | Director of the Center |\
| Board Directors | Administrative Board of the Center |\
| Advisory Board | Advisory Board of the Center |\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57314  Field of Activity\
The Center's activities include:\
- Research, analysis, implementation, and development in:\
  - EU law, institutions, and policies\
  - Integration of non-EU countries\
  - European politics, economics, history, and society\
- Cooperation with national/international organizations\
- Organization of conferences, congresses, and scientific meetings\
- Special courses for civil servants, academics, business, and civil community\
- Master and PhD programs\
- Consulting services for public/private organizations\
- Publication of "EUCES" international journal\
- Other duties assigned by the Rector\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56421  Administrative Structure\
#### Director\
- **Appointment:** By Rector for 3 years (renewable)\
- **Deputy:** May be appointed with Rector's approval\
- **Vacancy:** New Director appointed if position vacant for 3 months\
\
#### Director's Duties\
1. Chair Administrative and Advisory Boards\
2. Represent the Center\
3. Prepare work programs and activity reports\
4. Collaborate with scientific institutions\
5. Submit research results for Rector's approval\
6. Carry out other duties charged by Rector\
\
#### Administrative Board\
- **Composition:** 4 internal academic staff + Director\
- **Appointment:** By Rector for 3 years (renewable once)\
- **Meetings:** At least 2 times monthly\
- **Decision:** Absolute majority required\
\
#### Administrative Board Duties\
1. Determine Center's principles and work organization\
2. Evaluate appointed members\
3. Evaluate work programs and annual reports\
4. Decide on research, publications, and educational issues\
5. Form working groups/projects\
6. Decide on scientific and cultural activities\
\
#### Advisory Board\
- **Composition:** Minimum 7 specialists\
- **Appointment:** Elected by Administrative Board for 3 years\
- **Meetings:** At least once every 6 months\
- **Decision:** Absolute majority required\
\
#### Advisory Board Duties\
1. Help increase efficiency and productivity\
2. Make recommendations on new ideas and views\
3. Provide opinion on research and work strategy\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56508  Personnel and Finance\
- **Personnel:** Academic, administrative, and technical staff appointed by Rector\
- **Financing:** Financial management under Rector's competence\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56517  Implementation\
- **Entry into Force:** Date of publication\
- **Implementation:** Ensured by Rector\
\

- **Requirements:** At least PhD degree\

  - Use institution's equipment and infrastructure\
  - Participate in committees and activities\
  - Get qualified through existing projects\

  - Implement regulations\
  - Collaborate with colleagues and students\

1. Perform registration for new students\
2. Provide IDs and handle paperwork\
3. Prepare assessment reports\
4. Take over completed reports\
5. Give assessment information\

7. Maintain relationships with Dean's office and Scientific Committee\

### \uc0\u55356 \u57104  Language Requirements\
- **Primary Language:** English\
- **Exception:** Integrated law program offered in English and Albanian\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56541  Admission Requirements\
#### General Criteria\
- First or Second Cycle Diploma\
- Minimum CGPA determined by Decanate\
- English proficiency:\
  - MSc: Level B2 (CEFR)\
  - Professional Master: Level B1 (CEFR)\
\
#### English Proficiency Tests Accepted\
- Cambridge\
- IELTS\
- TOEFL (iBT/ITP)\
- TOEIC\
- GESE\
- APTIS\
- **Exemption:** University studies completed in English\
\
#### Admission Assessment\
- Based on:\
  - University study results\
  - Foreign language examination\
  - Interview/special skills test\
  - Reference letters\
  - Short essay on study reasons\
- **Missing Credits:** Up to 60 ECTS can be completed via adaptation courses\
- **Credit Recognition:** Up to 40 credits from previous activities\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56523  Registration Requirements\
#### Required Documents\
1. Completed application\
2. Notarized diploma copy\
3. Official transcript\
4. English proficiency proof\
5. ID card copy (passport for foreigners)\
6. Bank receipt for tuition\
7. 2 photos (4.5x6 cm)\
\
#### Student Status\
- Granted upon registration\
- Ends with: diploma, termination, expulsion\
- **Contract:** Study contract signed with University\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56424 \u8205 \u55356 \u57323  Academic Advisors\
- **Appointment:** By Scientific Committee\
- **Qualifications:** Lecturer with PhD degree\
- **Duties:**\
  - Consult on course selection\
  - Assist students with missing credits\
  - Provide career guidance\
  - Approve course registration\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56538  Attendance Requirements\
- **Lectures:** \uc0\u8805 60% attendance\
- **Practice/Seminars:** \uc0\u8805 75% attendance\
- **Laboratories:** \uc0\u8805 75% attendance\
- **Non-compliance:** Reported monthly to Department Coordinator\
- **Failed Courses:** Attendance not required for retakes\
\
### \uc0\u9203  Duration and Registration\
#### Study Duration\
| Program Type | Normal Duration | Maximum Duration |\
|-------------|-----------------|------------------|\
| MSc | 2 years (4 semesters) | 4 years (8 semesters) |\
| Professional Master | 1 year (2 semesters) | 2 years (4 semesters) |\
| Integrated Second Cycle | 5 years (10 semesters) | 10 years (20 semesters) |\
\
#### Course Registration\
- **Timing:** Before each semester per academic calendar\
- **Requirement:** Tuition payment completed\
- **Approval:** Required from academic advisor\
- **Maximum Load:** 45 ECTS per semester\
- **Consequences:** Cannot attend classes/exams without registration\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56580  Transfers\
- **Quotas:** Determined by Scientific Committee\
- **Requirements:**\
  - Complete at least one semester at EPOKA\
  - Apply within academic calendar timeframe\
  - Approval by Scientific Committee and Decanate\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56517  Academic Year Structure\
- **Semesters:** Fall and Spring (14 weeks + 2 exam weeks)\
- **Summer Semester:** Optional, requires approval\
- **Holidays:** No classes/exams on official holidays\
- **Weekend Classes:** Possible with Academic Senate approval\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56534  Educational Process\
#### Course Structure\
- **MSc:** 10-14 courses + thesis\
- **Professional Master:** 7-8 courses + microthesis\
- **Activities:** Lectures, projects, labs, practice, seminars\
\
#### Credit Systems\
- **American System:** Measures auditorium work only\
- **ECTS:** Measures total coursework including individual study\
- **Minimum:** 60 ECTS per academic year\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56541  Examination Procedures\
#### Exam Rights\
- **Prerequisite:** All financial liabilities redeemed\
- **Denial:** For unredeemed financial obligations\
\
#### Exam Process\
- **Schedule:** Announced at least 4 weeks in advance\
- **Format:** Normally written, verbal exams possible\
- **Evaluation:** By course lecturer(s)\
- **Absence:** Head of Department assigns substitute examiner\
\
#### Result Announcement\
- **Timing:** Per academic calendar\
- **Grade Appeal:** Within 5 days of announcement\
- **Appeal Process:** Reviewed by lecturer, finalized by Decanate within 15 days\
\

- **Thesis Eligibility (MSc):** CGPA \uc0\u8805 2.00, no FD/FF by end of 3rd semester\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57235  Thesis Requirements\
#### MSc Thesis\
- **Start:** After completing all courses (except professional practice)\
- **Defense:** After passing professional practice course\
- **Timing:** 4th semester\
\
#### Professional Master Microthesis\
- **Start:** 2nd semester\
- **Evaluation:** Graded with ECTS credits\
\
#### Thesis Procedures\
- **Supervisor Appointment:** By end of respective semester\
- **Topic Assignment:**\
  - Professional Master: By end of 1st semester\
  - MSc: By end of 3rd semester\
- **Registration:**\
  - MSc: Beginning of 4th semester\
  - Professional Master: Beginning of 2nd semester\
- **Supervisor Qualifications:** At least PhD degree\
\
#### Thesis Defense\
- **Jury:** \uc0\u8805 3 members + substitute\
- **Defense Duration:** 45-90 minutes\
- **Voting:** Closed doors, majority decision\
- **Possible Outcomes:**\
  - Accepted as is\
  - Accepted with minor corrections (30 days for submission)\
  - Conditionally accepted with major corrections (next regular semester for completion)\
  - Failed (new topic required)\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56520  GPA Calculations\
#### Semester GPA\
- Formula: \uc0\u931 (Grade \'d7 Credits) / Total Credits\
- Courses: Only current semester\
\
#### Cumulative GPA\
- Formula: \uc0\u931 (Grade \'d7 Credits) / Total Credits\
- Courses: All completed courses\
- Rounding: To two decimal places\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57286  Honors and High Achievers\
#### Honors Lists\
- **Honors:** CGPA 3.00-3.49\
- **High Honors:** CGPA 3.50-4.00\
- **Timing:** End of each semester\
\
#### High-Achieving Students\
- **Eligibility:** Successful completion of all previous courses\
- **Advanced Courses:**\
  - CGPA \uc0\u8805 3.00: One course from following year\
  - CGPA \uc0\u8805 3.50: Two courses from following year\
- **Approval:** Required from advisor\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56541  Additional Exams\
#### Eligibility\
- **Professional Master:** One failed course preventing graduation\
- **MSc:** One failed course preventing thesis start\
- **Integrated Second Cycle:** Max 2 failed courses or CGPA <2.00 with no failed courses\
\
#### Conditions\
- **Grades Eligible:** FF, FD, DD, DC\
- **Attempts:** Maximum 3 times\
- **Fees:** Free first attempt, fees for subsequent attempts\
- **CGPA Calculation:** Last grade received is valid\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57235  Graduation Requirements\
- **MSc:** Complete required credits + thesis (120 ECTS)\
- **Professional Master:** Complete required credits + microthesis (60 ECTS)\
- **General:** CGPA \uc0\u8805 2.00, no FD/FF grades\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56424 \u8205 \u55356 \u57235  Student Rights\
1. Clear and justifiable rules and procedures\
2. Protection from discrimination\
3. Access to academic information\
4. Use of institution's equipment, library, and internet\
5. Freedom of expression\
6. Participation in program evaluation\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56424 \u8205 \u55356 \u57235  Student Responsibilities\
1. Know and comply with regulations\
2. Maintain ethical and professional behavior\
3. Fulfill program requirements timely\
4. Contribute to positive institutional climate\
5. Pay fees on time\
6. Take responsibility for violations\
7. Compensate for material damages\
\
### \uc0\u9208 \u65039  Status Suspension\
#### Grounds for Leave\
1. Military service obligation\
2. Illness, natural disaster, arrest\
3. Economic/financial reasons\
4. Mental health conditions\
\
#### Leave Procedures\
- **Application:** Within 30 days of semester start\
- **Approval:** By Faculty Decanate\
- **Maximum Leave:** 2 semesters\
- **Fees:** Pay \'bc tuition for each semester on leave\
- **Extensions:** Possible with Decanate approval\
- **Exclusion:** Not counted in study duration\
\
#### During Leave\
- Cannot attend classes or take exams\
- No reimbursement for paid tuition\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u57002  Program Dismissal\
#### Voluntary Withdrawal\
- Submit written request to Registrar's Office\
- Termination after clearing all obligations\
- Receive transcript and submitted documents\
\
#### Involuntary Termination\
- Disciplinary sanctions\
- Exceeding maximum study duration\
- Based on "On Student Discipline" regulation\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56522  Quality Assurance\
- **Internal Evaluation:** Annual\
- **Accreditation:** Apply within legal timeframe\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57235  Diplomas\
#### Types Awarded\
- **Master of Science Diploma:** 120 ECTS + thesis\
- **Professional Master Diploma:** 60 ECTS + microthesis\
- **Integrated Second Cycle Diploma:** 300 ECTS + thesis\
\
#### Diploma Supplement\
- **Content:** Nature, cycle, content, results, regulations\
- **Design:** By Academic Senate per ministry guidelines\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56540  Implementation\
- **Effective Date:** Approval by Academic Senate\
- **Enforcement:** Ensured by Rector\

  10. Supervise research project administration\

  13. Archive project applications\

### \uc0\u55357 \u56496  Financial Management\
#### Support Structure\
- **Collaboration:** Research Office + University Finance Office\
- **Supervision:** Under University Administrator\
\
#### Budget Sources\
1. University scientific research and development budget\
2. External partners, donors, and project collaborators\
\
#### Budget Coverage\
- Faculty and research centre projects\
- International conference participation\
- Publication in indexed journals (Web of Science, Scopus, Avery)\
- Publication in other journals\
- Co-financing and participation in research projects\
\
#### Individual Budget Lines\
1. International conference participation\
2. Publication in indexed journals\
3. Publication in other journals\
4. **Regulation:** By "On the promotion of scientific publications" directive\
\
#### Staff Compensation\
- **Eligibility:** Full and part-time staff engaged in projects\
- **Calculation:** Project staff costs minus 30% institutional expenses\
- **Deductions:** Personal income tax and social security contributions\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56538  Publications Management\
#### Responsibility\
- **Primary:** Rector and Deans\
- **Collaboration:** With Research and Projects Office\
\
#### Regulations\
- **Classification:** By "On the promotion of scientific publications" directive\
- **Criteria:** By "On Publications" regulation\
\
#### Approval Process\
1. Submit to Research and Projects Office\
2. Prior information and evaluation by:\
   - Heads of Departments\
   - Faculty Deans\
   - Research Centre Directors (if applicable)\
3. Rectorate decides on financial allocation\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57286  Best Research and Researcher Award\
#### Process\
1. **Annual Analysis:** Academic Senate reviews research output\
2. **Shortlisting:** Best researchers selected\
3. **Recommendation:** To Permanent Commission on Quality Assurance\
4. **Evaluation:** By commission established annually\
5. **Reward Amount:** Determined by Rectorate\
\
#### Database\
- Research achievements recorded in research database\
- Used for annual evaluation\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56517  Implementation\

## 4. Regulation on Publications\
\

- **Publication Date:** 14.09.2007\
- **Pages:** 5\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57263  Scope\
- Covers textbooks and auxiliary textbooks published by University units\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56421  Publication Commission\
#### Formation\
- **Appointment:** By Administrative Board\
- **Members:** 2 Administrative Board members\
- **Chairmanship:** One of the Vice-Rectors\
\
#### Responsibilities\
1. Make necessary planning\
2. Take all publication decisions\
3. Supervise implementation\
4. **Effectiveness:** Decisions effective after Administrative Board approval\
\
#### Publication Evaluation Criteria\
1. Pedagogical qualifications and educational needs\
2. Appropriate category classification\
3. Print number, cost, sales price, and copyright price\
\
#### Support\
- May use teaching and administrative staff services\
- May establish temporary sub-committees\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56538  Publication Types\
#### Original Works\
- **Authors:** University members or external\
- **Requirement:** Commission decision after department opinion\
- **Authorization:** Written statement from author\
\
#### Translated Works\
- **Requirements:**\
  1. Written approval from translator\
  2. Written approval from copyright holder\
- **Fees:** Commission decides if paid from university budget\
\
### \uc0\u9997 \u65039  Author Responsibilities\
- **Scientific and Language Issues:** Responsibility of authors\
- **Submission:** Works delivered to Rector, Deans, and Research Centre Heads\
- **Department Review:** Required for works by lecturers, instructors, and teaching assistants\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56496  Copyright Payments\
#### Calculation Basis\
- **Formula:** Table points \'d7 Civil servant coefficient \'d7 Nominal pages\
- **Coefficient:** As defined in Budget Law\
\
#### Point Values\
| Publication Type | Copyright Points | Translation Points |\
|-----------------|-----------------|-------------------|\
| Textbooks | 175 | 125 |\
| Auxiliary Textbooks | 125 | 100 |\
| **Foreign Language Bonus:** +2 points |\
\
#### Page Calculation\
- **Standard:** 240 pages per hour per term\
- **Payment Basis:**\
  - <240 pages: Actual number of pages\
  - \uc0\u8805 240 pages: Maximum 240 pages paid\
- **Corrections:** By author/translator unless otherwise stated\
\
#### Additional Provisions\
- **Multiple Editions:** First edition payment covers \uc0\u8804 5000 copies\
- **Large Prints:** >5000 copies: +20% per page\
- **Extra Amounts:** Not printed without author authorization\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56516  Publication Costs\
#### Components\
1. Copyright\
2. Clich\'e9\
3. Paper\
4. Printing\
5. Binding\
6. Other expenses\
\
#### Calculation\
- **Cost per Book:** Total expenses \'f7 Number of prints\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56580  Subsequent Editions\
#### Contract Requirements\
- **First Edition:** Contract with author/translator\
- **Subsequent Editions:** New contract required for changes\
- **Failure to Contract:** Author/translator retains printing rights\
\
#### Payment Rates\
- **First Edition:** Full rate\
- **Subsequent Editions:** 3/5 of first edition rate\
- **New Sections:** First edition rate applies\
\
#### Small First Editions\
- **Condition:** <5000 copies\
- **Payment:** Withheld until subsequent editions complete 5000 copies\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56498  Sales and Pricing\
#### Sales Price Calculation\
- **Formula:** Material cost + 20%\
- **Rounding:** Fractions omitted or rounded up\
\
#### Student Discounts\
- **Minimum Discount:** 20%\

\
#### External Sales\
- **Permission:** By Publications Commission\
- **Minimum Price:** Not below printing cost\
\
### \uc0\u55357 \u56541  Printing Principles\
- **Standards:** Determined by Publications Commission\
- **Author Obligation:** Comply with principles if accepting university printing\
\
### \uc0\u55356 \u57217  Free Copies\

\
#### Distribution Copies\
